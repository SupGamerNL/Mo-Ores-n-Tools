Mo’ Ores ’n Tools

A Minecraft 1.21.1 Ore Expansion Mod for NeoForge & MCreator

About

Mo’ Ores ’n Tools adds new ores, tools, armor, and processing to Minecraft. This iteration focuses on simplicity, exploration, and meaningful progression.

Features

  • New ores with unique tool/armor stats
  • Biome-linked world generation
  • Simple processing items (hammer, press, etc.)
  • Decorative blocks & materials
  • No FE — crafted simplicity
